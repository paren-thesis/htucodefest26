About The Event

Innovating For The Future

What is COMPSSA Hack?

A flagship collaborative coding event uniting the brightest computer science students at Ho Technical University. Over a full week of building, teams design, prototype, and pitch inventive solutions from two real-world project briefs.

Who Can Participate?

Open to all students from Ho Technical University who are passionate about design, programming, or entrepreneurship. Teams of 4 – 5 members with at least one female in your team — beginners and experienced hackers alike are welcome!

Why Join Us?

Test your skills in a high-octane environment, receive dedicated mentorship from industry professionals, earn official certificates, network with developers, and grab your share of GHS 3,500+ in awards.

Hackathon Focus

Choose Your Track

Project 1

Live E-Voting System

Build a secure, real-time electronic voting platform suitable for student union elections or institutional polling — ensuring integrity, transparency, and accessibility.

  • Secure Voter Authentication
  • Real-Time Results Dashboard
  • Tamper-Proof Audit Trail
  • Mobile-Responsive Ballot Interface
Project 2

Dues Payment System

Design a streamlined dues collection and tracking platform for student associations — enabling members to pay, admins to manage records, and leadership to generate reports.

  • Member Payment Portal
  • Admin Dashboard & Records Management
  • Automated Payment Reminders
  • Financial Report Generation

Timeline

Event Schedule

June 12 - 15, 2026 (Friday - Monday)

Sign up online, form teams of 4 - 5 members, and secure your place in the hackathon.

Hackathon

Judging
